# Wanderly Travel Insurance Policy

## Plan Tiers and Pricing
Wanderly offers optional travel insurance at checkout in three tiers:
- **Essential** (~4% of trip cost): trip cancellation/interruption, emergency medical
- **Plus** (~6% of trip cost): everything in Essential, plus baggage delay/loss and travel delay reimbursement
- **Premium** (~8% of trip cost): everything in Plus, plus "cancel for any reason" (CFAR) coverage reimbursing up to 75% of trip cost, and higher medical/evacuation limits

Exact pricing is quoted per traveler based on trip cost, destination, and age at checkout.

## What's Covered
- Trip cancellation or interruption due to covered reasons (illness, injury, death of traveler or family member, natural disaster, airline-caused itinerary changes, and other reasons listed in the plan certificate)
- Emergency medical treatment and emergency medical evacuation while traveling
- Baggage delay (Plus and Premium), and baggage loss or damage (Plus and Premium)
- Travel delay reimbursement for meals and lodging when a covered delay exceeds 6 hours (Plus and Premium)
- Cancel for any reason, at 75% reimbursement, if purchased as Premium and selected within 14 days of initial trip deposit

## What's Not Covered
- Pre-existing medical conditions, unless the plan is purchased within 14 days of the initial trip deposit and the traveler is medically able to travel at time of purchase
- Cancellations due to a change of mind on Essential or Plus tiers (only Premium's CFAR add-on covers this)
- Extreme sports or professional athletic competition unless a rider is added
- Losses caused by traveling against an active government advisory issued before the policy was purchased
- Cash, jewelry, and electronics above the per-item limit listed in the plan certificate

## Filing a Claim
Claims are filed through the insurance partner's claims portal (a link is emailed with the policy confirmation) or by calling the 24/7 claims line printed on the policy documents. Required documentation typically includes receipts, airline/hotel confirmation of the covered event, and, for medical claims, an itemized bill. Claims should be filed within 90 days of the covered event; most claims are processed within 10-15 business days once complete documentation is received.

## How to Add Insurance
Insurance can be added at the time of booking or up to 24 hours after the initial deposit by contacting Wanderly support. Adding it later than 14 days after the initial deposit disqualifies the pre-existing-condition waiver and the CFAR add-on.
